B.Tech 1st Year Course Curriculum

Introduction

The Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) 1st year curriculum is designed course outcome and knowledge level core subjects include Maths, Physics, Chemistry, Programming, Communication Skills, Design Thinking, Mechanical, Electronics, Electrical Engineering, etc.

Semester-wise Subjects (Ordered List)

  1. Semester I Subjects.
    1. Mathematics
    2. Electronics Engineering
    3. Programming in cpp
    4. Communication Skills
    5. Design Thinking
    6. Mechanical Enginering

Programming in cpp include

  • Array
  • String
  • Loops
  • Programming Fundamentals
  • Conditional statements

Theory Subjects

Engineering Mathematics
Provides mathematical knowledge for engineering problem solving.
Design Thinking
Focuses on entrepreneurship and case study according to Stanford model.
Communication skills
Improves Listening, Speaking, Reading, Writing of a student.
